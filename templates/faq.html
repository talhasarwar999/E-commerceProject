FAQ'S

How far in advance should I place my order?

  • It is always in your favor to book an order as early as possible. We highly recommend placing orders at least 2-3 weeks in advance. If you have a customized order, book it at least 3-4 weeks before, so we can arrange it accordingly. Moreover, wait until your order is confirmed for its availability.

How do I place my order?

  • To place your order, go to our website (website address) and select your order. After choosing your order, you will be directed to the online order placement form, follow the instructions, and your order will be placed. Wait for the confirmation. In case of any confusions or queries, you can write to us at (email address) or call (phone number). We will be happy to serve you!

How do I pay?

  • We accept payments via credit cards, debit cards, or any internet banking service available. Once you place an order, you will be directed to a secure payment method.

What if I don’t know what to choose?

  • That’s nothing to worry about! Our staff would be happy to help you choose a cake flavor or flowers. They will also help you design a theme or coordinate the requirements with your event. Just email your queries at (email address) or call (phone number)!

What kind of events or occasions do you cover?

  • From weddings to birthdays to funerals, our products are available for any and every event. All the occasions where you may require cakes or flowers, we will be able to cater to your needs.

What if I have to cancel my order?

  • Any cancellation would be unfortunate of course; especially when we put our utmost efforts to complete an order. It may not be possible for us to accommodate any last minute cancellations, in every case. However, we may be able to accommodate some order cancellations with a reasonable notice. You need to get in touch and let us know as soon as possible. Our staff will help you with the cancellation process.

Do I get a refund?

  • At Dolce e Fiore, we give the best to make our products exceptional. But we understand, as humans, all of us can make some mistakes. If the problem occurs from our side, we will try to fix it. If the issues found are legit, we might offer a refund. However, we will be unable to offer a refund in some instances. For example, in case of a last minute cancellation, retainers will be non-refundable. Moreover, we cannot offer refunds for the following situations:
  • Weather related shipping problems
  • An incorrect or incomplete shipping/ delivery address
  • Products not removed from their packaging timely
